Collapsed View — 6 Concepts

Compact ways to show current watch + stretch progress in the collapsed pill — no long bar. Advance time or auto-play to compare the motion, then pick one.

Now:
1 · Radial Gauge
Ring split into 4 watches; active arc fills with stretch progress.
2 · Orbital
A glowing token orbits the day; 4 notches mark the watches.
3 · Quadrant Fill
2×2 of the day's watches; current cell fills like liquid.
4 · Horizon
Sun/moon travels a sky arc across the whole day.
5 · Dual Ring
Outer ring = watch; inner ring = stretch progress.
6 · Equalizer
4 bars for the watches; current bar rises with stretch.
