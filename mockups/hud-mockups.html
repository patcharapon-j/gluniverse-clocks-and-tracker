Clocks & Tracker — HUD Direction Previews

Four visual languages for the same data. Use the deck to advance time and watch each animate. Every HUD shows: weekday + day number + month + year, the active shift (with ambient tint), a tactile stretch meter (36 stretches per shift — no x/y readout), the hour, and a next-event chip. Default day model: 4 shifts × 6h, 10-min stretches.
