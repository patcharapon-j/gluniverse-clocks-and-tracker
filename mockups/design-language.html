GL Universe · Design Language · v1.0

Etched Glass

The unified GL Universe look: liquid glass (Clocks & Tracker's "Arcane Glass" material) as the surface, Arknights: Endfield precision (the Initiative module's drafting layer) etched on top of it. Frosted, blurred, glow-bloomed slabs of dark glass — annotated with chamfered corners, L-brackets, micro-tracked labels and 1px rules of light. Signal amber #FFD24A is the family's ceremony color.

Devices annotate like a technical drawing — quiet hairlines until their state matters. One cut corner per silhouette. Two stripe registers: opaque black×signal caution (mission edging, confirm) and translucent hazard red (doom, terminal) — ceremony only, never decoration. Print-press artifacts (crop marks, CMYK registration chips, serials) are Endfield's provenance garnish: at most one per window.

Digits exist as etched ghosts at 18% opacity; the signal-amber precision rule draws across and lights them as it passes — brightness bursts to 1.6, settles to 1.08. Anticipation ≤ 80ms, selective overshoot on numerals only. Ceremony color is signal amber regardless of accent context: system events outrank dispositions.
